The chart shows the number of administrative arrests made each fiscal year by Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE), excluding arrests made by Customs and Border Protection. 
The years included in the chart are FY 2009, 2011, 2013, 2015, 2017, and 2019.
The trend shows a peak in FY 2009 at 297,898 arrests, declining to 110,104 arrests in 2015 before rising again to 143,099 arrests in FY 2019.
