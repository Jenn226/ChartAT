The bar chart shows the percentage of American adults who owned each of seven devices in 2010. 
The devices are listed from most owned to least owned:
* Cell phone: 85%
* Desktop computer: 59%
* Laptop computer: 52%
* mp3 player: 47%
* Game console: 42%
* e-Book reader: 5%
* Tablet computer: 4% 
The data comes from a Pew Research Center survey of 3,001 adults.
