The chart, titled "Reasons people consider solar at home: Cost savings, environment," presents the results of a survey about why homeowners install solar panels. 
Here's the breakdown:
- **Saving Money:** The most common reason, with 96% of respondents citing it, is to save money on utility bills. 
- **Environmental Concerns:**  A close second, at 87%, is a desire to help the environment.
- **Solar Investment Tax Credit:** 67% of homeowners install solar panels to get the tax credit associated with a solar investment.
- **Health Benefits:**  Improving the health of themselves or their families motivates 60% of homeowners to install solar panels.
**Important Note:** The chart highlights that these results are based on homeowners who have already installed or seriously considered installing solar panels.
