The pie chart shows how Hispanic adults view their Hispanic background in terms of race and ethnicity.
* **Both, racial and ethnic background:** 56% of Hispanic adults believe that being Hispanic is part of both their racial and ethnic background.
* **Ethnic background:** 19% consider being Hispanic solely as part of their ethnic background.
* **Racial background:** 11% view it solely as a part of their racial background.
* **Don't know:** 13% are unsure whether their Hispanic background falls under race, ethnicity, or both.
The chart highlights that a majority of Hispanic adults see their heritage as encompassing both racial and ethnic dimensions.
