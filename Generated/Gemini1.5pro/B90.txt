The chart displays the percentage of people from different countries who believe that China plays a more important role in the world today than it did 10 years ago. 
Here's the breakdown:
- China: 70%
- Russia: 42%
- Germany: 35%
- U.S.: 31%
- India: 27%
- France: 22%
- UK: 21%
The data is based on a Spring 2018 Global Attitudes Survey by the Pew Research Center and represents the median responses from 25 countries surveyed.
