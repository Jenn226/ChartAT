The chart presents the results of a Spring 2019 Global Attitudes Survey about how Nigerians view China. It shows the percentage of Nigerians who:
* **Have a favorable view of China:** 70%
* **Say China's growing economy is a good thing for Nigeria:** 83%
* **Say investment from China is a good thing:** 82%
* **Have confidence in Chinese President Xi Jinping:** 61%
The data suggests that Nigerians generally have a positive view of China and its impact on their country.
