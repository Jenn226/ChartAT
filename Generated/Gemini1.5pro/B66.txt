A bar graph titled "Buddhists older than world's overall population" depicts the 2015 World median age by religion.
Buddhists, at 36 years old, had the highest median age.
Following Buddhists, in descending order of median age are:
* Jews: 37 years old 
* Unaffiliated: 36 years old 
* Other religions: 34 years old 
* Folk religions: 34 years old
* Christians: 30 years old
* Hindus: 27 years old
* Muslims: 24 years old 
The median age for all religions combined is 30 years old. 
Source: Pew Research Center demographic projections. "The Changing Global Religious Landscape"
