The chart describes the total deportations by Mexican immigration authorities in the first seven months (October-April) of each fiscal year from 2002 to 2019. 
Main points:
* Deportations peaked in fiscal year 2006 at 135,000. 
* There was a substantial decrease in deportations from 2006 to 2010, when they reached a low point of 33,000.
* Deportations increased between 2010 and 2016, peaking at 93,000 in 2016. 
* Since 2016, deportations have decreased but remain higher than the 2010 low.  
* The chart notes that the data for  April 2019 is incomplete.
