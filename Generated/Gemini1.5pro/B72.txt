The chart displays the results of a survey asking Pakistanis what they believe is the greatest national threat to their country. 
Here is the data breakdown: 
* **India:** 59%
* **The Taliban:** 23%
* **al Qaeda:** 4%
* **All/None of the above:** 7%
* **Don't know/Refused:** 7%
The data comes from the Spring 2012 Global Attitudes Survey, specifically question Q126, and is published by the Pew Research Center.
