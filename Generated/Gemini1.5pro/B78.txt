The chart describes the percentage of adult web users in the US who use various social media websites. Data is from March/April 2014.  It shows:
* **YouTube and Facebook are most popular:**  Around 6-in-10 (63% and 77% respectively) of web users utilize these platforms. 
* **Significant drop for other platforms:** LinkedIn, Google Plus, and Twitter lag significantly behind, used by 25%, 24%, and 21% of adults respectively.
The data highlights that in 2014, a clear dominance of YouTube and Facebook existed in the social media landscape amongst adult U.S. web users.
