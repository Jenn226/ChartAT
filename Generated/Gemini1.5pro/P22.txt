This is a pie chart showing how often Twitter users check for material posted by others. 
* **Several times a day:** 24%
* **Once a day:** 21%
* **1-2 days per week:** 20%
* **3-5 days per week:** 12%
* **Every few weeks:** 7%
* **Less often:** 5%
* **Never:** 12% 
The data is taken from a Pew Research Center survey conducted in November 2010 among 2,257 adult internet users in the US.
