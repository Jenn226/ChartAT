The chart shows the results of a survey asking Indians what they perceive to be the greatest national threat to their country. The responses are as follows:
* **Pakistan:** 41%
* **Lashkar-e-Taiba:** 19%
* **Naxalites:** 16%
* **China:** 5%
* **All/None of the above:** 3%
* **Don't know/Refused:** 17%
The data comes from the Pew Research Center's Spring 2012 Global Attitudes Survey.
