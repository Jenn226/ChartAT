The chart shows the world population from 1950 projected to 2100.
Key observations:
- The global population in 1950 was 2.5 billion.
- By 2020, this number had grown to 7.8 billion.
- Projections show the population will reach 10.9 billion by 2100.
- While population continues to increase, the rate of growth is slowing down, indicated by the flattening curve in the latter half of the projected timeline.
