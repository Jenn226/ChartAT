This is a pie chart illustrating the results of a survey about the reasons for the rise in gas prices. 
Here's a breakdown:
* **Greed/Oil companies/Speculation:** 31% of respondents believe this is the main reason for increased gas prices.
* **Wars/Mideast unrest:** 19% attribute the rise to wars and unrest in the Middle East.
* **No opinion:** 14% of respondents have no opinion on the matter.
* **Politics/Policy:** Another 14% believe politics and policy are the driving force behind rising gas prices.
* **Economic forces:** 12% attribute the increase to economic factors.
* **Other reasons:** The remaining 10% cite other reasons for the price hike.
