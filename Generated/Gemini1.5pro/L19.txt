This line chart from Pew Research Center illustrates the estimated total number of unauthorized immigrants in the United States from 1990 to 2017.
**Key Findings:**
* **Increasing Trend:** The number consistently rose from 3.5 million in 1990 to a peak of 12.2 million in 2007.
* **Peak and Decline:** After reaching its highest point in 2007, the total number of unauthorized immigrants decreased, settling at 10.5 million in 2017.
* **Confidence Interval:** The chart uses shading to indicate the range of estimates within a 90% confidence interval. 
**Data Source:** The data is based on Pew Research Center estimates derived from augmented U.S. Census Bureau data.
