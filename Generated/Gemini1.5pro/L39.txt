The line chart shows that the average length of time unauthorized immigrant adults have lived in the U.S. has been increasing. 
More specifically:
- In 1995, the median was around 7.1 years. 
- This number increased steadily to 15.1 years by 2017
- The sharpest increase occurred between 2010 (10.6 years) and 2015 (14.8 years)
