The chart shows what percentage of US adults thought police spending in their area should be increased, decreased, or stay about the same in June 2020.  
* A plurality, 42%, thought it should stay about the same. 
* 31% thought it should be increased...
* ...while 25% thought it should be decreased.  
* Of those who thought it should be increased: 
    * 11% thought it should be increased "a lot". 
    * 20% thought it should be increased "a little". 
* Of those who thought it should be decreased:
    * 12% thought it should be decreased "a lot".
    * 14% thought it should be decreased "a little".
