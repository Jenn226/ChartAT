The chart claims that the majority of people believe that there is no general agreement on what is considered "racist language" and "sexist language.
Specifically:
* **61%** believe people do not generally agree on what is considered **racist language.**
* **65%** believe people do not generally agree on what is considered **sexist language.**
Additionally, the chart highlights that **48%** find it hard to know what others might find offensive. 
It is sourced from a Pew Research Center survey of U.S. adults conducted between April 29 and May 13, 2019.
