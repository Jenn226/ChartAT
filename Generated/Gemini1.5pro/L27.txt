The chart is a line graph that shows the percentage of American adults aged 18+ who owned a tablet computer from May 2010 to May 2013.
**Key Findings:**
* **Sharp Increase in Ownership:** Tablet ownership increased dramatically between 2010 and 2013. 
* **Steady Growth:** The growth was fairly steady, with more significant jumps occurring between February 2012 and April 2012, and again from August 2012 to May 2013.
**Data Points:**
* **May 2010:**  3%
* **May 2013:** 34%
**Other Details:**
* **Source:** Pew Research Center's Internet & American Life Project
* **Survey Dates:** May 2010 - May 2013
* **Margin of Error:** +/- 2.3 percentage points
