This line chart shows the percentage of voters in the US who cast their ballots by mail over a 20 year period. 
In 1996, 7.8% of voters cast their ballot by mail. This steadily rose over the next two decades:
* 10.1% in 2000
* 12.9% in 2004
* 16.4% in 2008
* 18.5% in 2012 
* 20.9% in 2016
 The trend clearly shows an increasing popularity for postal voting in the United States.
