This is a bar chart illustrating the results of a Pew Research Center Internet Project Survey conducted January 9-12, 2014, of 1006 adults. 
**The chart shows the percentage of adults who said specific technologies would be very hard or impossible to give up.**
Here's the breakdown:
* **Internet:** 46%
* **Cell phone:** 44% 
* **Television:** 35% 
* **Email:** 34%
* **Landline telephone:** 17%
* **Social media:** 10%
The chart indicates that the internet and cell phones were considered the hardest technologies to give up, while social media was seen as the easiest to give up.
