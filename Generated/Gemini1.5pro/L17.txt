The chart shows the percentage of eligible U.S. voters who are Latino from 2000 to 2020. 
**Key takeaways:**
* The share of Latino eligible voters has been steadily increasing.
* In 2000,  7.4% of eligible voters were Latino. 
* By 2020, that share is projected to reach 13.3%. 
**Other details:**
* The chart uses data from the Pew Research Center.
*  The 2020 data point is a projection based on U.S. Census Bureau data. 
* The chart specifies that an "eligible voter" is a U.S. citizen aged 18 or older. 
* It also clarifies that Latinos can be of any race.
