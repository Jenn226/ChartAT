The line chart demonstrates the percentage of Americans from different political affiliations who held favorable views of NATO from 2009 to 2020. 
**Key Observations:**
* **Democrats consistently more favorable:** Throughout the period, Democrats (blue line) consistently held more favorable views of NATO compared to Republicans (red line).
* **Peak favorability for Democrats:** Democratic favorability peaked in 2017 and 2018, reaching 74% and 76% respectively. 
* **Low point for Republicans:** Republican favorability reached its lowest point in 2013 at 43%. 
* **Converging trends in 2020:** Notably, both groups showed increasing favorability towards NATO from 2019 to 2020, with a significant jump for Democrats and a slight increase for Republicans.
