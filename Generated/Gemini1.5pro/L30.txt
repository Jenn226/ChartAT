The chart, titled "Very unfavorable views of Pakistan increasing in India," shows the percentage of Indians who hold very unfavorable views of Pakistan has trended upwards from 2013 to 2017. 
Here are the specific data points:
* **2013:**  54%
* **2014:** 49%
* **2015:** 51%
* **2016:** 55%
* **2017:** 64%
The data is sourced from the Pew Research Center's Spring 2017 Global Attitudes Survey.
