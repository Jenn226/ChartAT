This is a bar chart showing the percentage of US adults surveyed in 2019 who believe the federal government is doing too little to protect specific environmental concerns.
* **Protecting water quality of lakes, rivers, and streams:** 68%
* **Protecting air quality:** 67%
* **Reducing the effects of global climate change:** 67%
* **Protecting animals and their habitats:** 62%
* **Protecting open lands in national parks and nature preserves:** 55%
