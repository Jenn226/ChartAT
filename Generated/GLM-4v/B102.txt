The chart depicts the percentage of Democratic or Lean Democratic voters who have a positive impression of the candidates running for the Democratic nomination, categorized by different time periods:
1. Jan 2020: 64%
2. Jan 2008: 78%
3. Jan 2004: 46%
4. Feb 1992: 45%
5. Jan 1988*: 37%
The data source is from a survey conducted by the Pew Research Center between January 6-19, 2020. The note at the bottom indicates that surveys in 2008 and before were conducted over the phone, and the sample for January 1988 included all adult Democrats and Democratic leaners.
