The chart is a bar graph from the Pew Research Center that represents the percentage of adult Web users who use various social networking sites. The data is based on a survey conducted between March 19-April 29, 2014. It lists five different platforms:
1. Facebook - 77%

3. LinkedIn - 25%
4. Google Plus - 24%
