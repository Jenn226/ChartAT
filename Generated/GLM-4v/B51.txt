The chart depicts the number of reported anti-Semitic acts in France from the year 2011 to 2018. Each bar represents a different year, with the height indicating the count of incidents for that particular year. For instance, in 2011 there were 615 reported acts, while in 2018 there were 541 reported acts. The data is sourced from the Commission Nationale Consultative des Droits de l'Homme (CNCDH) and the Ministère de l'Intérieur, France. Additionally, the chart includes a note stating that the figures include acts against people and property.
