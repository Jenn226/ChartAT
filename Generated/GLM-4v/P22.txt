The image is a pie chart that represents how often Twitter users check for material posted by others. The percentage of Twitter users who check several times a day is 24%, those who do so once a day are 12%, and there's an additional 12% checking 1-2 days per week. A smaller group checks 3-5 days per week, making up 7%. Only 5% check less often, and 21% never check for other people's posts on Twitter.
The source of this data is cited as The Pew Research Center's Internet & American Life Project from their November 3-24, 2010 Post-Election Tracking Survey. This survey included 2,257 adult internet users ages 18 and older, with 755 cell phone interviews conducted in English and Spanish.
