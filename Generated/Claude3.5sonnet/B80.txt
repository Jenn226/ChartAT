This chart shows survey results about Nigerians' views on China. It includes four specific statements with corresponding percentages:
1. 70% of Nigerians have a favorable view of China.
2. 83% say China's growing economy is a good thing for Nigeria.
3. 82% say investment from China is a good thing.
4. 61% have confidence in Chinese President Xi Jinping.
The chart is titled "Nigerians generally express positive views of China" and presents the data in horizontal bar format. The source is cited as the Spring 2019 Global Attitudes Survey by the Pew Research Center. Overall, the data indicates that a majority of Nigerians hold positive opinions about China and its economic influence on Nigeria.
