The line graph shows that from 2013 to 2017, India's unfavorable view towards Pakistan has been on an upward trend. In 2013, it was at about 54%, and by 2017, this number had increased to over 64%. The y-axis represents "Very unfavorable views of Pakistan", while the x-axis indicates different years ranging from 2013 to 2017. This data is sourced from Spring 2017 Global Attitudes Survey conducted by PEW Research Center.
