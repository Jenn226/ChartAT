The bar graph shows that among U.S. adult social media users who say they changed their views on political or social issues because of something they saw on social media, about one in ten (9%) said it was due to President Donald Trump and his policies. This is followed by race relations in general at 11%, with Black Lives Matter movement coming next at 12%. The other categories include police brutality/need for reform (also 12%), political parties/ideologies/politicians (11%), social issues in general (9%), and coronavirus-related information (8%). It's important to note that multiple responses were allowed so some respondents may have selected more than one category.
