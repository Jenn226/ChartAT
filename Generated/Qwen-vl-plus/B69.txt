The bar graph shows the number of refugees resettled by state in fiscal year 2019, rounded to the nearest hundred. The y-axis represents each U.S. state and the x-axis indicates the total number of refugees settled there during this period. Texas is at the top with over 2500 refugees followed closely by Washington (around 1900), New York (approximately 1800) and California (also around 1800). Kentucky, Ohio, North Carolina, Arizona, Georgia and Michigan have similar numbers ranging from about 1400-1300 respectively.
