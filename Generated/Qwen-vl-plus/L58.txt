The graph shows that both Republicans and Democrats have consistently expressed an unfavorable view towards Iran over time, with slight fluctuations in their percentages. In 2009, around 85% of Republicans had an unfavorable opinion about Iran compared to approximately 73% for Democrats. By 2020, these numbers remained relatively stable at roughly 83% for Republicans and 65% for Democrats. The data is sourced from the Summer 2020 Global Attitudes Survey by PEW Research Center (Q8f).
