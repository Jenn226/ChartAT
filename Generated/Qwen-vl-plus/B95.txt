The bar graph shows support for democratic principles in different countries, with "Fair judiciary" having the highest percentage at 82%, followed by "Gender equality" and then other items such as "Free religion", "Regular elections", etc., all above 60%. The percentages shown here represent the median based on data from 34 countries worldwide. This information is sourced from Spring 2019 Global Attitudes Survey conducted by PEW Research Center. The title of this survey report is “Democratic Rights Popular Globally but Commitment to Them Not Always Strong”.
