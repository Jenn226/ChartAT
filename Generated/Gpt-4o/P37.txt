The chart titled "Six-in-ten in U.S. say scientists should take an active role in policy debates" presents the results of a survey conducted by Pew Research Center from January 7-21, 2019. The chart specifically shows the percentage of U.S. adults' opinions on scientists' roles in public policy debates about scientific issues.
The pie chart is divided into three sections:
- 60% of respondents believe that scientists should "take an active role in policy debates."
- 39% of respondents believe that scientists should "focus on establishing sound scientific facts."
- 1% of respondents did not provide an answer.
