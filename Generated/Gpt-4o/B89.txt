The chart provides information from the Spring 2019 Global Attitudes Survey conducted by the Pew Research Center, highlighting the pessimism among South Koreans about their political system. The specific content of the chart includes:
- 68% of South Koreans were pessimistic about the way their political system works.
- 62% of South Koreans disagreed that most elected officials care about what people like them think.
- 49% of South Koreans disagreed that the state is run for the benefit of the people.
- 44% of South Koreans were dissatisfied with Korean democracy.
