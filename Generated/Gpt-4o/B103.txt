The chart from the Pew Research Center depicts the perceived difficulty of telling parents about sexual orientation or gender identity among LGBT individuals. It includes the following statistics:
- **Telling mother was...**
- Not difficult: 40%
- Difficult: 59%
- **Telling father was...**
- Not difficult: 34%
- Difficult: 65%
The chart notes that these percentages are based on LGBT individuals who have told their mother or father about their sexual orientation or gender identity. The sample size is 648 individuals for mother and 435 for father. Responses from individuals who did not answer the questions are not shown.
