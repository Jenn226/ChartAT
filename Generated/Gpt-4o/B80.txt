The chart presents the results of a survey by the Pew Research Center, measuring Nigerian attitudes toward China. The survey tracks four specific questions, and the percentages of Nigerians who gave positive responses are indicated:
1. **70%** of Nigerians have a favorable view of China.
2. **83%** of Nigerians believe that China's growing economy is beneficial for Nigeria.
3. **82%** of Nigerians think that investment from China is a good thing.
4. **61%** of Nigerians have confidence in Chinese President Xi Jinping.
The data comes from the Spring 2019 Global Attitudes Survey by Pew Research Center, referring specifically to questions Q8b, Q28, Q38b, and Q41.
