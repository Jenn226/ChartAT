The chart depicts the share of Indians who expressed various concerns related to Pakistan, Kashmir, and terrorism prior to recent tensions. The specific concerns and the percentage of Indians who agree with each are as follows:
1. **Pakistan is a very serious threat to our country** - 76%
2. **Terrorism is a very big problem** - 65%
3. **Indian government should use more military force in Kashmir** - 58%
4. **The situation in Kashmir is a very big problem** - 55%
5. **The situation in Kashmir has gotten worse over the past five years** - 53%
The information is based on the Spring 2018 Global Attitudes Survey conducted by Pew Research Center among Indian adults from May 23 to July 23, 2018.
