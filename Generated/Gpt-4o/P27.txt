The chart is a pie chart titled "Nigerians unsatisfied with how their democracy works." It displays responses to the question: "How satisfied are you with the way democracy is working in our country?"
The chart has three segments with the following percentages:
- "Not satisfied" is represented in blue, occupying 60% of the pie.
- "Satisfied" is represented in green, occupying 39% of the pie.
- "Don't know" is represented in a very small light green segment, occupying 1% of the pie.
The source of this data is a survey of Nigerian adults conducted from June 25 to July 30, 2018, Q4, by PEW Research Center.
