The chart shows the percentage of American adults who owned various gadgets in 2010. The gadgets listed and their corresponding ownership percentages are as follows:
- **Cell phone:** 85%
- **Desktop computer:** 59%
- **Laptop computer:** 52%
- **mp3 player:** 47%
- **Game console:** 42%
- **e-Book reader:** 5%
- **Tablet computer:** 4%
The source of the data is the Pew Research Center's Internet & American Life Project, from a survey conducted between August 9 and September 13, 2010. The survey included a sample size of 3,001 adults aged 18 and older, including 1,000 reached via cell phone. Interviews were carried out in both English (2,804 respondents) and Spanish (197 respondents).
