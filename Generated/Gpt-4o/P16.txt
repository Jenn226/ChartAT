The chart from the Pew Research Center provides information on the number of ad preference categories listed for U.S. adult Facebook users. The percentages are as follows:
- 27% of users have fewer than 10 categories.
- 27% of users have between 10 to 20 categories.
- 33% of users have 21 or more categories.
- 11% of users are not assigned to a category.
- 2% of users did not give an answer.
The data was collected from a survey of Facebook users conducted between September 4 and October 1, 2018, as part of the study "Facebook Algorithms and Personal Data."
