The chart is a pie chart presenting the results of a survey conducted among Democrats and Democratic-leaning independents. The chart addresses the question: "Would you like to see other Democratic candidates challenge Barack Obama for the party's nomination for president in 2012?"
The specific content of the chart is as follows:
- 59% responded "No, do not challenge" (in dark blue).
- 32% responded "Yes, challenge Obama" (in light blue).
- 9% responded "Don't know" (in gray).
The source of the chart is the Pew Research Center in collaboration with The Washington Post.
